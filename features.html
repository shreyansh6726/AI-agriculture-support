Everything Farmers Need, in One Platform

We designed AI Agriculture Support with continuous feedback from farmer producer organisations, agri-experts, and field officers across India.

Farm Profiles

Capture geolocation, soil type, irrigation, and crop history so the platform understands each farm’s context.

  • Secure farmer accounts
  • Multi-plot support
  • Offline data sync

Predictive Advisory

Machine learning models blend weather, soil, and market signals to recommend crop rotations that improve profitability.

  • Season-wise plans
  • Localised sowing dates
  • Yield projections

Disease Diagnostics

Detect crop diseases from images and receive mitigation plans compliant with agri-extension norms.

  • Image-based detection
  • Pest lifecycle tracking
  • Safe treatment guides

Smart Inputs

Recommend fertilisers, micronutrients, and pesticides aligned with crop stage, soil health, and regional guidance.

  • Nutrient budgeting
  • Dosage calculators
  • Organic alternatives

Voice-first Support

  • Speech-to-text engine
  • Instant voice playback
  • Nine Indian languages

Community Insights

Grounded in Local Knowledge

Farmers store their locations once and unlock geographic analytics tailored to their village. The system factors in soil organic carbon, rainfall patterns, and irrigation coverage to prepare resilient crop plans.

  • GIS pinning with satellite overlays
  • KPIs for water balance, soil moisture, and NDVI
  • Works with manual or sensor-based inputs

AI Crop Planner

Our crop planning model compares historical yield data, input costs, and farm conditions to suggest the best varieties for each season. Recommendations include sowing windows, intercrop ideas, and risk warnings for extreme weather.

Model Inputs

Soil fertility score Seven-day weather outlook Commodity trends Local pests risk index

Diagnose & Act Fast

Farmers upload pictures of affected crops via mobile or kiosk. The model identifies diseases, grades severity, and recommends treatments aligned with agricultural university advisories.

  • Confidence score with explainable outputs
  • Stepwise treatment workflow
  • Push notifications for follow-ups

Voice AI Concierge

Farmers can tap the mic and start a natural conversation. The assistant understands intent, fetches relevant insights, and plays back recommendations instantly in the farmer’s chosen language.
